About me.

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in maths at the University of Vienna. I am part of the FWF Project Waves in Population Genetics whose PI is Emmanuel Schertzer.

I did my Ph.D. at the École Polytechnique, CMAP, under the supervision of Pascal Maillard & Gaël Raoul. The manuscript of my thesis, entitled Spatial dynamic of interfaces in ecology: deterministic and stochastic models can be found here.

My research is in probability theory. My work focuses on microscopic models for front propagation (in particular branching systems) and their applications to ecology and population genetics. More generally, I am interested in the impact of structure on the genetic diversity of a population.

Julie TOURNIAIRE

About me.

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Evolutionary genetics group (Barton group) at the IST Austria and in the Biomathematics group (in the group of Emmanuel Schertzer) at the University of Vienna. Before this, I did my Ph.D. at the École Polytechnique, CMAP, under the supervision of Pascal Maillard & Gaël Raoul. The manuscript of my thesis, entitled Spatial dynamic of interfaces in ecology: deterministic and stochastic models can be found here.

I am a mathematician. My research is in probability theory. My work focuses on microscopic models for front propagation and their applications to ecology and population genetics.
